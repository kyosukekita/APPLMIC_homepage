The BACELL meeting 2020 will be held at The Kobe Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI) , Kobe, Japan.

A Brief Introduction to Kobe

Location: Midwest Japan, Next to Osaka and Kyoto
Population: 1.5 million, 6th largest city in Japan
Average Temp (Apr): 19 ºC / 66.2 ºF～ 10 ºC / 50 ºF
Compact, convenient cosmopolitan city in a natural setting (mountains & ocean) Mt. Rokko, Osaka Bay & Inland Sea etc.

International Access

①Fly to Knasai International Airport (KIX)
  • 2nd biggest int’l airport in Japan
  • 1,126 int’l flights/week from 65 cities around the world

    • ②Transfer to Kobe by sea or road
  • 30 min transfer from Kansai Int’l via high speed ferry (1 Bay Shuttle per hour) or
  • 60 min transfer via bus. (One bus every 30 mins during peak hours)


    • Proposed Venue & Date

    Venue: The Kobe Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI) and Ariston Hotel (connected to KCCI)
    Date: 19th - 22nd April 2020 (19th reception/20th-21st conference/22nd excursion)

    Easy access - Located on Port Island, a man made island directly in front of downtown Kobe.
    Meeting rooms are located on the 3rd floor of the Kobe Chamber of Commerce and Industry while poster sessions and exhibition take place on the 2nd floor.
    All dining and function will take place in the adjacent Ariston Hotel Kobe which connects to the chamber of commerce via undercover walkway to make for a convenient and compact venue space.
