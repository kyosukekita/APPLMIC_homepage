BACELL 2020

19th-22nd April in Kobe, Japan

Since the B. subtilis genome sequencing project meeting in Paris in 1990, the BACELL meeting series has been held annually as an international conference to discuss academic, industrial and technological development of studies on B. subtilis and its related organisms in Europe.

Call for Abstract (New Deadline 13th December 2019)
If there is a special reason, we will consider to accept late submissions until the end of January 2020, Please contact us by email at bacell2020@outlook.jp.
For Japanese participants: You are encouraged to submit the abstract as you usually do for the domestic annual Gram+ meeting serise.

Registration page is now open.

A Brief Introduction to Kobe

Location: Midwest Japan, Next to Osaka and Kyoto
Population: 1.5 million, 6th largest city in Japan
Average Temp (Apr): 19 ºC / 66.2 ºF～ 10 ºC / 50 ºF
Compact, convenient cosmopolitan city in a natural setting (mountains & ocean) Mt. Rokko, Osaka Bay & Inland Sea etc.

    Venue: The Kobe Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI) and Ariston Hotel (connected to KCCI)

    Easy access - Located on Port Island, a man made island directly in front of downtown Kobe.
